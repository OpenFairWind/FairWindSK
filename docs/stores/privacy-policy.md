# FairWindSK privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-07-23

FairWindSK is an open-source, vessel-local marine Multi-Functional Display and
Signal K application host.

## Data handled by the app

FairWindSK may process location, vessel telemetry, alarms, waypoints, routes,
regions, notes, charts, tracks, files, server credentials, and configuration
chosen by the operator. It uses this information to provide the features
requested by the operator and to communicate with Signal K servers and web
applications selected by the operator.

FairWindSK does not include an advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or developer-
operated cloud collection service in this repository. The app does not sell
personal data.

## Network services and third-party content

The operator chooses the Signal K server, web applications, URLs, and native
Android applications used with FairWindSK. Those services and applications may
process data under their own terms and privacy policies. FairWindSK cannot
control the behavior of independently operated services.

## Device permissions

FairWindSK may request location and network access where needed for marine
positioning, local service discovery, and connection to operator-selected
services. Android launcher integration queries launchable activities only and
does not request broad package visibility.

## Storage and deletion

Configuration and locally managed resources are stored on the device or on the
operator-selected Signal K server. Operators can remove local app data through
the operating system and can manage remote resources through the applicable
server.

## Children

FairWindSK is a professional and recreational marine operations tool and is not
directed to children.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact details in the
[FairWindSK support page](./support.md).
