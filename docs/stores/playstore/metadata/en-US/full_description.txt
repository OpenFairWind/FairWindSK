FairWindSK is a touch-first marine Multi-Functional Display and Signal K application host for your vessel.

ONE HELM, ONE CLEAR WORKSPACE

Keep live marine instruments, vessel operations, configuration, and embedded Signal K web applications inside a consistent single-window interface designed for use at the helm.

MARINE INSTRUMENTS

Monitor position, course and speed over ground, heading, speed through water, depth, waypoint information, bearing, distance, ETA, cross-track error, velocity made good, connection health, and more.

YOUR SIGNAL K APPLICATIONS

Discover compatible applications from your selected Signal K server, organize them into pages and sub-pages, and launch them from the FairWindSK workspace.

ANDROID LAUNCHER OPTION

On Android 13 and newer, FairWindSK remains a normal app and can optionally be selected as the Home app. You decide which installed launchable activities join the same application palette as Signal K apps. Installation never changes the default Home app automatically.

VESSEL DATA AND SAFETY

Work with waypoints, routes, regions, notes, charts, tracks, and files. Use Person Over Board, anchor, alarm, and compatible autopilot workflows from the shared operational shell.

BUILT FOR TOUCH

Large controls, high-contrast presentation, and Default, Dawn, Day, Sunset, Dusk, and Night comfort presets support clear operation in changing light.

FairWindSK requires access to a compatible Signal K server for server-backed data and applications. Feature availability depends on the server, installed plugins, connected sensors, and operator configuration.

FairWindSK is not a certified navigation, collision-avoidance, or lifesaving device. Always maintain a proper lookout and use approved charts, instruments, and safety procedures.
